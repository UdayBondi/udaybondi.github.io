Uday Bondi

I am a final year undergraduate student at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, where I work on deep learning for medical image analysis under the supervision of Prof. R Krishna Kumar.

I am currently working on Super Resolution of medical images under the guidance of Prof. Jinman Kim and Prof. R Krishna Kumar. Previously, I worked as an undergraduate researcher at the Advanced Geometric Computing Lab on sketch simplification with Prof. M Ramanathan Muthuganapathy. In the summer of 2019, I was fortunate to work at BMIT research group in School of Computer Science, The University of Sydney under the guidance of Prof. Jinman Kim and Dr. Ralph Nannan.
